Ajman Media City (AMC)

Ajman Media City (AMC) is a modern and cost-effective free zone tailored for creative, media, and service-oriented businesses. AMC provides a business-friendly environment with affordable setup costs and flexible packages, making it ideal for startups, SMEs, and entrepreneurs in media, technology, and consultancy sectors.

Steps to Form a Company in Ajman Media City (AMC)

1

Choose a Business Activity:

AMC supports activities such as media production, marketing, IT services, e-commerce, consulting, and more.
Select the activity that aligns with your business objectives.

2

Select a Legal Structure:

Free Zone Establishment (FZE): For a single shareholder.
Free Zone Company (FZC): For multiple shareholders.
Branch Office: For an existing local or foreign company.

3

Select a License Type:

Media License: For creative and media-related activities.
E-commerce License: For online businesses.
Service License: For consultancy and professional services.

4

Reserve a Trade Name:

Choose a unique name that complies with AMCâ€™s naming regulations.

5

Submit the Initial Application:

Provide details of the business, including trade name, activity, and shareholder information.

6

Prepare Required Documentation:

Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
Passport-sized photographs.
Proof of residential address (e.g., utility bill).
No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).

7

Select a Business Package:

AMC offers flexible packages, including virtual offices, shared desks, and dedicated offices, depending on your requirements.

8

Pay the Fees:

Pay the registration and licensing fees as per the chosen package and business activity.

9

Obtain the Business License:

Upon approval, AMC issues the trade license and incorporation documents.

10

Open a Corporate Bank Account:

Use the issued company documents to open a UAE corporate bank account.

11

Apply for Visas:

AMC provides visa services for investors, employees, and dependents based on your package.
