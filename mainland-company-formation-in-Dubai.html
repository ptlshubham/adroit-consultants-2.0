Mainland Company

Dubai is a global hub for business and investment, making it an attractive destination for entrepreneurs looking to establish a mainland company. A Dubai Mainland company allows businesses to operate across the UAE and internationally without restrictions. Below is an overview of the services typically involved in forming a mainland company in Dubai

Business Setup Solutions

Step-by-Step Assistance

1

Legal Structure & Business Activity

  • Consultation to identify the best legal structure (e.g., LLC, Sole Establishment, Branch Office).
  • Guidance on selecting a business activity from the Department of Economic Development (DED) list.

2

Trade Name Registration

  • Assistance in choosing a unique and compliant trade name.
  • Registration of the trade name with the DED.

3

Local Sponsor or Service Agent

  • Arranging local Emirati sponsors for LLCs (51% ownership).
  • Appointing a local service agent for 100% foreign-owned businesses.

4

Office Space & Tenancy Contract

  • Help in securing a physical office space as per DED requirements.
  • Assistance with drafting and notarizing the tenancy contract (Ejari).

5

Initial Approval from Authorities

  • Submission of documents for preliminary approval from the DED.
  • Coordination with relevant authorities if additional approvals are required.

6

Documentation & Notarization

  • Drafting and notarizing the Memorandum of Association (MOA) or Local Service Agent Agreement.
  • Preparation of all required legal documents.

7

Trade License Issuance

  • Applying for and obtaining the business license from the DED.
  • Includes payment of license fees and related costs.

8

Visa & Immigration Services

  • Assistance with investor, partner, and employee visas.
  • Support in medical, Emirates ID, and labor card processes.

9

Bank Account Opening

  • Guidance in choosing a UAE-based bank.
  • Assistance with compliance requirements for opening a corporate bank account.

10

PRO Services

  • Handling government-related processes like visa renewals and trade license updates.

11

Post-Formation Support

  • VAT registration assistance.
  • Ongoing advisory services for compliance and renewal of licenses.
