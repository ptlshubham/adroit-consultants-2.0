GOLDEN VISA IN UAE

Your Gateway to UAE Residency

The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residency visa introduced in 2019 to attract and retain talented individuals, investors, and entrepreneurs. It offers a 5- or 10-year renewable residency without the need for a local sponsor. Designed to boost economic growth and innovation, the visa allows holders to live, work, or study in the UAE, offering benefits like 100% business ownership and family inclusivity. It targets key groups, including investors, entrepreneurs, skilled professionals, researchers, and outstanding students, creating opportunities in a secure and globally connected environment.

Advantages of a UAE Golden Visa

WHY IT MATTERS

Long-Term Residency

Provides 5 or 10 years of residency, renewable automatically, for investors, entrepreneurs, and specialized professionals.

No Need for a Local Sponsor

Holders can live, work, or study in the UAE without requiring sponsorship from a local employer or individual.

Full Ownership of Business

Allows 100% ownership of businesses on the mainland of the UAE, removing the requirement for a local partner.

Family Benefits

Includes family members (spouse, children) and allows dependents to stay until the end of their permit, even after the visa holder's death.

Enhanced Lifestyle

Access to world-class healthcare, education, and infrastructure in a secure and vibrant environment.

Residency for Top Talents

Facilitates entry for outstanding students, researchers, doctors, innovators, and artists.

Ease of Travel

Facilitates travel in and out of the UAE with a multiple-entry visa.

Your Guide to the Golden Visa Process

Steps to apply for a UAE Golden Visa

Navigating the UAE Golden Visa process is simple and rewarding. Whether you're an investor, entrepreneur, or skilled professional, understanding the eligibility and application steps ensures a smooth journey. Follow these key steps to secure long-term residency in the UAE.

1 - Determine Eligibility

Golden Visa categories include:

• Investors in public investments or real estate.

• Entrepreneurs.

• Specialized talents like doctors, scientists, researchers, creatives, and outstanding students.

2 - Prepare Required Documents

• Valid passport and UAE visa (if applicable).

• Proof of eligibility (e.g., business ownership, academic qualifications, patents, or investment proof).

• Good conduct certificate from relevant authorities.

• Health insurance (specific to UAE standards).

3 - Submit an application

• Apply via the Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship (ICA) or the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) online portals.

• Visit service centres if an in-person submission is required.

4 - Verification and Approval

• Authorities review documents and verify eligibility.

• Processing times may vary depending on the category of application.

5 - Complete Medical Exam

• Undergo a UAE-standard medical fitness test, including X-rays and blood tests.

6 - Obtain the Golden Visa

• Once approved, you'll receive the Golden Visa sticker on your passport, or it may be linked digitally.
