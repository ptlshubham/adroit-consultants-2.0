The Hub for Media, Creativity & Innovation Since 2001.

Dubai Media City (DMC), established in 2001, is a thriving free zone tailored for media, advertising, and creative businesses. Strategically located in Dubai, DMC provides a supportive ecosystem for media and communication companies, offering state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities.

Steps to Form a Company in Dubai Media City (DMC)

1

Choose a Business Activity:

  • Dubai Media City supports various activities such as media production, advertising, publishing, broadcasting, and consultancy services.
  • Select an activity that aligns with your business objectives.

    • 2

    Select a Legal Structure:

  • Free Zone Limited Liability Company (FZ-LLC): Suitable for single or multiple shareholders.
  • Branch Office: For companies expanding into the UAE.

    • 3

    Choose a Business License Type:

  • Media License: For activities related to media production, publishing, and broadcasting.
  • Consultancy License: For professional and advisory services.
  • Event Management License: For organizing events and exhibitions.

    • 4

    Reserve a Trade Name:

  • Choose a unique trade name following DMCâ€™s naming regulations.

    • 5

    Submit the Initial Application:

  • Provide details about your business activity, shareholders, and proposed trade name.

    • 6

    Prepare Required Documents:

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of residential address (e.g., utility bill).
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).

    • 7

    Lease an Office Space:

  • DMC offers a range of office solutions, including flexi-desks, coworking spaces, and fully furnished offices.

    • 8

    Pay the Fees:

  • Pay the registration and licensing fees based on your chosen business activity and office requirements.

    • 9

    Obtain the Business License:

  • Upon approval, DMC issues the business license and Certificate of Incorporation.

    • 10

    Open a Corporate Bank Account:

  • Use the company documents to set up a corporate bank account in the UAE.

    • 11

    Apply for Visas:

  • DMC provides visa services for investors, employees, and dependents.

    • Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Dubai Media City (DMC)

    • Strategic Location: Located in Dubai, close to major business hubs, transport links, and key media outlets.
    • 100% Foreign Ownership: Full ownership of the business without needing a local sponsor.
    • No Taxes: 0% corporate tax, personal income tax, and no customs duties.
    • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: World-class facilities and media-centric infrastructure.
    • Wide Range of Business Activities: Ideal for media production, publishing, broadcasting, consultancy, and event management.
    • Global Networking Opportunities: Access to a vibrant community of media professionals and industry events.
    • Flexible Office Solutions: Options include flexi-desks, coworking spaces, and dedicated offices.
    • Repatriation of Profits: 100% repatriation of capital and profits.
    • Visa and Residency Benefits: Investor, employee, and dependent visa options tailored to your business needs.
    • No Currency Restrictions: Freedom to transact in multiple currencies without limitations.
    • Ease of Business Setup: Streamlined registration process with quick approvals.
    • Supportive Ecosystem: Access to media-focused legal, business, and talent acquisition services.
    • Collaborative Environment: Opportunity to collaborate with leading media and advertising companies.
    • No Physical Office Requirement (for Virtual Offices): Virtual offices allow businesses to establish a presence without a physical office.
    • Access to Talent: Proximity to a pool of skilled professionals in media and creative industries.

    Popular Business Activities in Dubai Media City

    1. Media Production: Film, video, and photography production.
    2. Advertising and Marketing: Creative and digital advertising services.
    3. Publishing: Magazines, books, and online content publishing.
    4. Broadcasting: Radio and television production and transmission.
    5. Event Management: Organizing events, exhibitions, and conferences.
    6. IT and Consultancy Services: Technology and business consulting for media projects.

    Dubai Media City is a leading free zone for media and communication businesses in the UAE. Its cutting-edge infrastructure, vibrant business community, and strategic location make it an ideal base for companies aiming to grow in the Middle East and global markets. Whether you are a media entrepreneur or an established organization, DMC offers unparalleled opportunities to thrive in the competitive media landscape.
