Formation of a Company in Dubai World Trade Center (DWTC) Business Setup

Dubai World Trade Center (DWTC) is one of the UAE’s most prestigious business hubs, strategically located in the heart of Dubai. As a free zone, DWTC offers an exceptional environment for companies to thrive, especially for those in trade, exhibitions, events, and Corporates. Established as a key center for international business, DWTC hosts major global trade shows and offers advanced facilities for a wide range of industries, making it a prime location for company setup

Steps to Set Up a Company in Dubai World Trade Center (DWTC)

  1. Choose a Business Activity: DWTC supports a diverse range of business activities such as trading, exhibition organizing, event management, consulting, and various other Corporates.
  2. Select a Legal Structure:

    o Free Zone Establishment (FZE):For a single shareholder.
    o Free Zone Company (FZC): For multiple shareholders.
    o Branch Office: For existing companies wishing to establish a branch in DWTC.

  3. Submit the Application: Complete the application form, specifying the business activity, legal structure, and required documents.
  4. Reserve a Trade Name: Choose a unique trade name for your company in accordance with DWTC’s naming regulations.
  5. Prepare Required Documents:

    o Passport copies of shareholders, managers, and directors.
    o Proof of address.
    o Business plan (if required).
    o No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).

  1. Choose Office Space: DWTC offers various office solutions, including serviced offices, commercial spaces, and event venues, depending on the business’s requirements.
  2. Pay Fees: Submit the payment for registration, licensing, and office space fees as per the selected business structure and office space.
  3. Obtain the License: Once your application is approved and fees are paid, DWTC will issue your business license and certificate of incorporation.
  4. Open a Corporate Bank Account: Open a bank account for your company using the official company documents provided by DWTC.
  5. Apply for Visas: Depending on the type of business, apply for investor, employee, and dependent visas.

Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Dubai World Trade Center (DWTC)

Strategic Location s

  • Situated in the heart of Dubai, DWTC is a prime location, offering easy access to major business districts, luxury hotels, and commercial centers. The proximity to key transport hubs like Dubai International Airport adds to its accessibility.

100% Foreign Ownership

  • Companies in DWTC are allowed to have 100% foreign ownership, offering complete control to investors without the need for a local partner or sponsor.

Tax Exemptions

  • 0% corporate tax for up to 50 years (renewable).
  • 0% personal income tax for employees.
  • No customs duties for goods imported into the free zone.
  • No restrictions on capital or profit repatriation.

World-Class Infrastructure

  • DWTC offers state-of-the-art office spaces, conference centers, and event venues, providing businesses with cutting-edge facilities that meet global standards. It is a hub for international corporations and industries.

Access to Global Markets

  • DWTC is a key business hub that connects companies to global markets, especially through the Dubai International Convention and Exhibition Centre. This is ideal for companies that wish to expand internationally and network with global clients and partners.

Business Flexibility

  • DWTC offers various license options, including for trading, consultancy, exhibitions, and Corporates, ensuring businesses can operate in line with their activities. It also provides flexibility in setting up your legal structure, such as a Free Zone Establishment or Branch Office.

Networking Opportunities

  • DWTC is home to numerous international businesses and hosts major trade events, creating ample networking and collaboration opportunities with companies across various sectors.

Support for Global Expansion

  • DWTC’s international connectivity and access to the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Europe markets make it an ideal platform for businesses looking to expand globally.

No Currency Restrictions

  • There are no currency restrictions for businesses in DWTC, which allows for smooth international transactions and the flexibility to deal in any currency.

Easy Setup Process

  • The company setup process in DWTC is quick, efficient, and hassle-free, offering a smooth experience for entrepreneurs and businesses aiming to establish operations in Dubai.

Visa and Residency Options

  • DWTC provides various visa options for investors, employees, and dependents, along with the ability to sponsor families, making it easier to attract and retain talent from around the world.

State-of-the-Art Technology and Services

  • Companies benefit from the latest in office technologies, telecom infrastructure, and business services, which can help enhance operational efficiency and competitiveness.

No Minimum Capital Requirement

  • There is no minimum capital requirement for company formation in DWTC, making it a more affordable option for entrepreneurs and small businesses.

Access to Skilled Workforce

  • DWTC’s location in the center of Dubai provides businesses with easy access to a diverse and highly skilled workforce, facilitating recruitment and talent acquisition.

Long-Term Lease Options

  • DWTC offers long-term lease options for office spaces, ensuring stability and cost-effectiveness for companies looking for a long-term base in Dubai.

Proximity to Major Hotels and Commercial Districts

  • Being close to luxury hotels, retail outlets, and Dubai's commercial districts, DWTC offers businesses an ideal place to establish a presence and attract international clients and partners.
