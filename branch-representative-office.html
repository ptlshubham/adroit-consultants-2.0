Grow Globally with a UAE Branch Office

Looking to expand your business presence in the UAE? Setting up a Branch or Representative Office in the Mainland offers incredible opportunities for global companies to access the vibrant UAE market and beyond.

Why Choose a Branch/Representative Office?

  • 100% Foreign Ownership – Retain full control of your business.
  • Tax Benefits – Enjoy the UAE’s tax-friendly environment.
  • Market Expansion – Leverage Dubai's strategic location to access global markets.
  • Government Contracts – Qualify to work on lucrative projects with UAE authorities.
  • No Capital Requirement – Focus your investment on operations and growth.

Key Differences

Branch Office

Engage in activities identical to the parent company, including profit-making operations.

Representative Office

Focus on marketing and promotion without direct profit-making activities.
