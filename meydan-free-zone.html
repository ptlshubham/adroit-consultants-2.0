Meydan Free Zone (MFZ)

Meydan Free Zone, located in Dubai, offers a prime location and innovative infrastructure for businesses. Situated near Downtown Dubai and key transport links, it is ideal for entrepreneurs, startups, and established companies seeking a strategic base for operations.

Steps to Form a Company in Meydan Free Zone

Meydan Free Zone supports various activities, including trading, consultancy, e-commerce, and service-oriented businesses.
Ensure your chosen activity aligns with the free zone’s regulations.

  • Free Zone Limited Liability Company (FZ-LLC): Single or multiple shareholders.
  • Branch Office: For an existing local or foreign company.

  • Choose a unique name that meets Meydan Free Zone’s guidelines and reflects your business activity.

Provide preliminary business details, including shareholder and activity information.

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of address (e.g., utility bill).
  • Business plan (for certain activities).
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if required).

Meydan Free Zone offers flexible leasing options such as virtual offices, coworking spaces, and dedicated offices.

Pay the registration, licensing, and office rental fees based on your chosen package and activity.

Once the application is approved, Meydan Free Zone issues the business license and Certificate of Incorporation.

Use your company documents to open a corporate bank account in the UAE.

Apply for investor, employee, and dependent visas based on the size of your office and business requirements.
