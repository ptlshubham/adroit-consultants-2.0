Meydan Free Zone (MFZ)

Meydan Free Zone, located in Dubai, offers a prime location and innovative infrastructure for businesses. Situated near Downtown Dubai and key transport links, it is ideal for entrepreneurs, startups, and established companies seeking a strategic base for operations.

Steps to Form a Company in Meydan Free Zone

  • Select a Business Activity: Meydan Free Zone supports various activities, including trading, consultancy, e-commerce, and service-oriented businesses. Ensure your chosen activity aligns with the free zone’s regulations.
  • Choose a Legal Structure: Free Zone Limited Liability Company (FZ-LLC): Single or multiple shareholders. Branch Office: For an existing local or foreign company.
  • Reserve a Trade Name: Choose a unique name that meets Meydan Free Zone’s guidelines and reflects your business activity.
  • Choose a Legal Structure: Free Zone Limited Liability Company (FZ-LLC): Single or multiple shareholders. Branch Office: For an existing local or foreign company.
  • Submit Initial Application Provide preliminary business details, including shareholder and activity information.
  • Prepare Required Documentation Passport copies of shareholders and directors. Passport-sized photographs Proof of address (e.g., utility bill). Business plan (for certain activities). No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if required).
  • Lease a Business Space Meydan Free Zone offers flexible leasing options such as virtual offices, coworking spaces, and dedicated offices.
  • Pay the Fees Pay the registration, licensing, and office rental fees based on your chosen package and activity.
  • Obtain the Trade License Once the application is approved, Meydan Free Zone issues the business license and Certificate of Incorporation.
  • Open a Corporate Bank Account Use your company documents to open a corporate bank account in the UAE.
  • Apply for Visas Apply for investor, employee, and dependent visas based on the size of your office and business requirements.
