Steps to Form a Company in Sharjah Media City (Shams)

1

Select a Business Activity:

  • Shams allows over 120 business activities, including media, trading, consulting, and technology services.
  • Choose single or multiple activities under one license.

    • 2

    Choose a Legal Structure:

  • Shams LLC (Limited Liability Company): For businesses with individual or corporate shareholders.
  • Branch Office: For existing companies seeking expansion into the UAE.

    • 3

    Register a Trade Name:

  • Choose a unique trade name that complies with Shams’ naming rules and standards.

    • 4

    Submit an Online Application:

  • Provide basic details, including:
  • Business activity.
  • Shareholder and director information.
  • Proposed trade name.

    • 5

    Submit Required Documents:

  • Basic documents include:
  • Passport copy of the shareholder(s).
  • Passport-sized photograph.
  • Proof of residential address (e.g., utility bill).
  • Emirates ID and visa copy (for UAE residents).

    • 6

    Office Space Options:

  • Shams offers flexible workspace solutions, including:
  • Virtual offices.
  • Shared desks.
  • Dedicated office spaces.

    • 7

    Receive Initial Approval:

  • Upon document submission, you’ll receive initial approval for the company.

    • 8

    License Issuance:

  • Pay the required fees to obtain your business license.
  • Shams does not require physical presence to complete the setup process, making it convenient for foreign investors.

    • 9

    Open a Bank Account:

  • Use your business license and corporate documents to open a bank account with a UAE-based bank.

    • 10

    Apply for Visas:

  • Shams allows visa applications for investors, employees, and dependents.

    • Advantages of Setting Up a Company in DMCC

    • 100% Foreign Ownership: Complete ownership without the need for a local sponsor.
    • Cost-Effective Setup: Shams is one of the most affordable free zones in the UAE with low setup and renewal costs.
    • Flexible Licensing Options: Combine multiple business activities under one license.
    • Quick Setup Process: Fast and straightforward company registration with minimal paperwork.
    • No Physical Presence Required: Foreign investors can establish a company remotely without visiting the UAE.
    • Tax Benefits: 0% corporate tax and personal income tax.
      Exemption from import/export duties.
    • Repatriation of Profits: 100% repatriation of profits and capital.
    • No Office Requirement: Shams provides virtual office options, allowing businesses to operate without a physical office.
    • Wide Range of Business Activities: Includes media production, advertising, trading, e-commerce, consulting, IT, and more.
    • Visa Flexibility: Affordable visa packages for investors, employees, and dependents.
    • No Annual Audit Requirement: Shams companies are not required to submit annual audits or financial reports.
    • Strategic Location: Located in Sharjah, close to key logistics hubs, ports, and airports, providing excellent connectivity.
    • Networking Opportunities: A creative hub for media, technology, and content-focused industries fosters collaboration and innovation.
    • Support for Creative Industries: Tailored services for media production, advertising, and publishing businesses.
    • No Share Capital Requirement: Shams does not mandate any minimum paid-up share capital for company formation.

    Popular Business Activities in Shams

    1. Media and Advertising: Media production, digital marketing, and creative content creation.
    2. E-commerce: Online retail and trading.
    3. Consulting Services: Business advisory, IT consulting, and project management.
    4. Technology: Software development and tech startups.
    5. Trading: Import/export and wholesale distribution.

    Why Choose Sharjah Media City (Shams)?

    Shams offers a highly flexible and cost-efficient platform for businesses, particularly for entrepreneurs and startups in media, technology, and creative industries. With simplified processes, strategic location, and excellent tax benefits, Shams is a top choice for investors looking to establish or expand their presence in the UAE.
