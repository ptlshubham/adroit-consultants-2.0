Formation of a Company in Sharjah Airport International Free Zone

Sharjah Airport International Free Zone (SAIF Zone) is one of the most prominent and established free zones in the UAE. It is strategically located next to Sharjah International Airport and offers a wide array of business opportunities for entrepreneurs and investors. Known for its excellent logistics and proximity to both air and sea transport hubs, SAIF Zone is ideal for companies engaged in trading, manufacturing, logistics, and services. The setup process in SAIF Zone is streamlined and provides various benefits for businesses looking to establish a presence in the UAE.

Steps to Set Up a Company in Sharjah Airport International Free Zone

  • Select Your Business Activity: SAIF Zone allows a wide variety of business activities, including trading, manufacturing, consultancy, logistics, e-commerce, and more. Choose an activity that aligns with your business goals.
  • Choose a Legal Structure:
    ⦁ Free Zone Establishment (FZE): For a company with a single shareholder.
    ⦁ Free Zone Company (FZC): For a company with multiple shareholders.
    ⦁ Free Zone Company (FZC): Branch Office:For foreign companies wishing to establish a branch in SAIF Zone.
  • Trade Name Reservation: Choose a unique trade name that complies with SAIF Zone’s naming guidelines and reflects the nature of your business.
  • Submit the Application: Complete the application form and submit it along with the required documents. These typically include passports of shareholders and managers, proof of address, and trade activity details.
  • Prepare Required Documents:
    ⦁ Passport copies of shareholders, directors, and managers.
    ⦁ Proof of address of shareholders and directors.
    ⦁ Business plan (if required).
    ⦁ No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the current employer (for UAE residents).
  • Prepare Required Documents:
    o Passport copies of shareholders, directors, and managers.
    o Proof of address of shareholders and directors.
    o Business plan (if required).
    o No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the current employer (for UAE residents).
  • Select Office Space: SAIF Zone offers various office space options, from flexi-desks and serviced offices to larger warehouse facilities, depending on your business requirements.
  • Pay Fees: Pay the registration and licensing fees based on the selected business structure and office space.
  • Obtain Your License: Upon approval of your application and payment of fees, SAIF Zone will issue your business license, allowing you to legally operate within the free zone.
  • Open a Corporate Bank Account: Once your company is licensed, you can open a corporate bank account with one of the UAE’s banks.

Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Sharjah Airport International Free Zone

  1. Strategic Location: Located next to Sharjah International Airport and in close proximity to Sharjah Port, SAIF Zone provides easy access to key international markets, making it ideal for trading, logistics, and aviation-related businesses.
  2. 100% Foreign Ownership: o SAIF Zone offers 100% foreign ownership, allowing businesses to operate independently without the need for a local sponsor or partner.
  3. Trade Name Reservation:
    o 0% corporate tax for up to 50 years (renewable).
    o 0% personal income tax for owners and employees.
    o No customs duties on imports and exports within the free zone.
    o Full repatriation of profits and capital.
  4. World-Class Infrastructure: SAIF Zone offers state-of-the-art facilities, including offices, warehouses, and industrial spaces that meet international business standards. This infrastructure is well-equipped for various business sectors.
  5. Easy Setup Process: The company formation process in SAIF Zone is straightforward and fast, allowing businesses to get operational quickly.
  6. Proximity to Key Transport Hubs: Being close to Sharjah International Airport and the Sharjah Port provides businesses with excellent access to both air and sea freight, ensuring efficient logistics and import/export operations.
  7. Flexible Business Licensing: SAIF Zone offers flexibility in business licensing, allowing you to choose from various licenses for trading, manufacturing, service-based companies, and more.
  8. No Currency Restrictions:Businesses in SAIF Zone can operate in any currency, facilitating smoother international transactions.
  9. Visa Options: SAIF Zone provides visa options for investors, employees, and dependents, offering flexibility in staffing your business operations.
  10. Cost-Effective Setup: SAIF Zone is known for its affordable setup options compared to other free zones in the UAE, making it a great choice for startups and small businesses.
  11. Supportive Business Ecosystem: The free zone offers a range of services, including business support, marketing assistance, and networking opportunities, helping businesses grow and expand in the UAE.
  12. Easy Access to Skilled Labor: SAIF Zone is located within the emirate of Sharjah, providing access to a large pool of skilled labor, making it easier for businesses to hire the right talent.
  13. 24/7 Operations: The free zone supports round-the-clock business operations, which is ideal for logistics, trading, and e-commerce businesses that require flexibility in timing.
  14. Proximity to Major Markets: SAIF Zone’s location makes it ideal for businesses that wish to target the growing markets in the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, along with easy access to Europe and other global regions.
  15. Business Networking Opportunities:As one of the busiest free zones in the UAE, SAIF Zone provides ample networking opportunities with other businesses, facilitating collaboration and partnerships.
