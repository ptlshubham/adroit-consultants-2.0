Your gateway to affordable success!

Ajman Free Zone (AFZ) is one of the UAE's most affordable and business-friendly free zones, offering excellent opportunities for startups, SMEs, and multinational corporations. Its strategic location near Ajman Port and proximity to Dubai and Sharjah make it an ideal choice for businesses focusing on trade, manufacturing, and services.

Steps to Form a Company in Ajman Free Zone (AFZ)

1. Select a Business Activity: AFZ supports a wide range of activities, including trading, e-commerce, services, and industrial activities.

2. Choose a Legal Structure:

Free Zone Establishment (FZE): For a single shareholder.

Free Zone Company (FZC): For multiple shareholders (up to 5).

Branch Office: For an existing local or foreign company.

3. Choose a Business License Type:

o Trading License: For import/export and wholesale trading.

o Service License: For professional and consultancy services.

o Industrial License: For manufacturing and industrial operations.

o E-commerce License: For online businesses.

o General Trading License: For a broader range of trading activities.

4. Reserve a Trade Name:
Choose a unique trade name following AFZ’s naming guidelines.

5. Submit Initial Application:

Provide basic business information, including chosen activity, trade name, and shareholder details.

6. Prepare Required Documents:

Passport copies of shareholders and directors.

Passport-sized photographs.

Proof of residential address (e.g., utility bill).

No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).

7. Lease Office or Warehouse Space:

o AFZ offers flexible workspace options:

o Smart offices.

o Executive offices.

o Warehouses for industrial and trading companies.

8. Pay the Required Fees:
Pay the license and office leasing fees based on the business type and requirements.

9. Obtain the Business License:
Upon approval, AFZ issues the business license and Certificate of Incorporation.

Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Ajman Free Zone (AFZ)

  • 100% Foreign Ownership: Full ownership of the company without a local sponsor.
  • Cost-Effective Setup: AFZ is one of the most affordable free zones in the UAE, with competitive license and renewal fees.
  • Tax Benefits: 0% corporate tax, personal income tax, and import/export duties.
  • Strategic Location: Proximity to Ajman Port, Sharjah International Airport, and major UAE markets.
  • Wide Range of Business Activities: Suitable for trading, e-commerce, services, and industrial sectors.
  • Flexible Office Solutions: Virtual offices, smart desks, and executive offices for businesses of all sizes.
  • Repatriation of Profits: 100% repatriation of profits and capital.
  • No Currency Restrictions: Freedom to transact in multiple currencies without limitations.
  • Ease of Doing Business: Simple and streamlined company formation process with minimal documentation.
  • Visa and Residency Options: Affordable visa packages for investors, employees, and dependents.
  • Modern Infrastructure: Well-equipped office spaces, warehouses, and industrial facilities.
  • Customs and Trade Facilitation: Simplified customs processes for importing and exporting goods.
  • No Annual Audit Requirement: Small businesses are exempt from submitting annual financial audits.
  • E-commerce-Friendly: Specialized licenses for online businesses, including platforms and marketplaces.
  • Networking Opportunities: Access to a vibrant business community and support services.
  • Support for Industrial Businesses: Dedicated facilities and incentives for manufacturing and production companies.

Popular Business Activities in Ajman Free Zone

  1. Trading: Import/export and wholesale trading.
  2. E-commerce: Online retail and product marketplaces.
  3. Consultancy Services: Business advisory and management services.
  4. Industrial Activities: Manufacturing and light assembly.
  5. Logistics and Warehousing: Storage and distribution services.

Why Choose Ajman Free Zone (AFZ)?

Ajman Free Zone is an excellent choice for entrepreneurs, startups, and SMEs looking for a cost-effective business setup with excellent connectivity and access to major UAE markets. Its simple processes, modern infrastructure, and strategic location make it a preferred free zone for diverse business sectors. Whether you're entering the UAE market for the first time or expanding your operations, AFZ provides the flexibility and tools to ensure your success.
