Free Zone

Strategic, Cost-Effective, and Global

Hamriya Free Zone (HFZ), located in Sharjah, is one of the most prominent free zones in the UAE. Known for its strategic location, world-class facilities, and cost-effective business setup options, it is particularly suited for trading, manufacturing, logistics, and industrial businesses. HFZ provides a robust platform for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to establish a presence in the UAE with easy access to global markets.

Steps to Form a Company in Hamriya Free Zone (HFZ)

1

Choose a Business Activity:

  • HFZ offers a variety of business activities, including trading, manufacturing, import/export, warehousing, logistics, and services.
  • Select an activity that suits your business objectives.
    • 2

    Select a Legal Structure:

  • Free Zone Establishment (FZE): A single shareholder company.
  • Free Zone Company (FZC): Multiple shareholders (a partnership structure).
  • Branch Office: For an existing company looking to expand its operations in the UAE.
    • 3

    Reserve a Trade Name:

  • Choose a unique name that aligns with HFZ's naming regulations and your business activity.
    • 4

    Submit the Initial Application:

  • Submit a detailed application including business activity, legal structure, and shareholders' information.
    • 5

    Prepare Required Documentation:

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of residential address.
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).
    • 6

    Choose an Office Space:

  • HFZ offers various office options, including flexi-desks, warehousing space, and custom-built offices.
    • 7

    Pay the Fees:

  • Pay the registration, license fees, and office rental fees as per your selected package and business activity.
    • 8

    Obtain the Trade License:

  • Once the application is approved, HFZ will issue the business license and incorporation documents.
    • 9

    Open a Corporate Bank Account:

  • Use the company documents to open a corporate bank account in the UAE.
  • Select an activity that suits your business objectives.
    • 10

    Apply for Visas:

  • Depending on the office space and package, apply for visas for investors, employees, and dependents.

    • Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Hamriya Free Zone (HFZ)

    Setting up a company in Hamriyah Free Zone (HFZ) offers 100% foreign ownership, tax exemptions, and full repatriation of profits. Its strategic location and world-class infrastructure make it ideal for international trade and manufacturing.

    Strategic Location:

  • HFZ is strategically located with easy access to key shipping routes, and is close to major seaports (Sharjah Port) and airports (Sharjah International Airport), offering excellent connectivity for international trade.

    • Cost-Effective Setup:

  • One of the most affordable free zones in the UAE, ideal for startups, SMEs, and small businesses looking for cost-effective setup options.

    • Variety of Business Activities:

  • HFZ caters to a broad spectrum of industries, including trading, manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, and services.

    • Visa and Residency Benefits:

  • HFZ offers visa options for investors, employees, and dependents, with flexibility in the number of visas based on office space and business activity.

    • No Annual Auditing Requirement for SMEs:

  • Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are generally not required to submit annual audits, reducing administrative burden.

    • 100% Foreign Ownership:

  • No need for a local sponsor or partner, offering complete ownership and control of your business.

    • Flexible Business Setup Options:

  • HFZ offers flexible business licenses and customized solutions, including single-owner companies and multi-shareholder partnerships.

    • Easy Setup Process:

  • Streamlined procedures with a simple and quick application process for business setup and licensing.

    • Access to a Global Network:

  • Businesses in HFZ benefit from access to a large network of international clients, suppliers, and service providers, boosting business growth opportunities.

    • Proximity to UAE Business Hubs:

  • Being close to Dubai and Sharjah offers businesses access to major markets, infrastructure, and clients in the UAE and across the GCC region.

    • Tax Exemptions:

  • HFZ offers several tax benefits, including:
  • 0% corporate and personal income tax.
  • 0% customs duties on imports and exports.
  • No restrictions on repatriation of profits or capital.

    • No Currency Restrictions:

  • Businesses can operate in any currency without restrictions, allowing for smoother international transactions.

    • Warehousing and Industrial Facilities:

  • HFZ is known for its robust infrastructure for warehousing and manufacturing, providing businesses with customized facilities for production, storage, and distribution.

    • Full Repatriation of Profits and Capital:

  • There are no restrictions on the repatriation of profits, capital, or dividends, offering financial flexibility for investors.

    • International Shipping and Logistics Hub:

  • The proximity to Sharjah Port and other key ports makes HFZ ideal for import/export and logistics companies, with easy access to shipping routes.
