1

Choose a Business Activity:

  • Select an activity that suits your business objectives.
    • 2

    Select a Legal Structure:

  • Free Zone Establishment (FZE): A single shareholder company.
  • Free Zone Company (FZC): Multiple shareholders (a partnership structure).
  • Branch Office: For an existing company looking to expand its operations in the UAE.
    • 3

    Reserve a Trade Name:

  • Choose a unique name that aligns with HFZ's naming regulations and your business activity.
    • 4

    Submit the Initial Application:

  • Submit a detailed application including business activity, legal structure, and shareholders' information.
    • 5

    Prepare Required Documentation:

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of residential address.
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) for UAE residents (if applicable).
    • 6

    Choose an Office Space:

  • HFZ offers various office options, including flexi-desks, warehousing space, and custom-built offices.
    • 7

    Pay the Fees:

  • Pay the registration, license fees, and office rental fees as per your selected package and business activity.
    • 8

    Obtain the Trade License:

  • Once the application is approved, HFZ will issue the business license and incorporation documents.
    • 9

    Open a Corporate Bank Account:

  • Use the company documents to open a corporate bank account in the UAE.
  • Select an activity that suits your business objectives.
    • 10

    Apply for Visas:

  • Depending on the office space and package, apply for visas for investors, employees, and dependents.

    • Strategic Location: HFZ is strategically located with easy access to key shipping routes, and is close to major seaports (Sharjah Port) and airports (Sharjah International Airport), offering excellent connectivity for international trade.
    • 100% Foreign Ownership: No need for a local sponsor or partner, offering complete ownership and control of your business.
    • Tax Exemptions: 100% repatriation of profits and capital without restrictions.

      o 0% corporate and personal income tax.

      o No restrictions on repatriation of profits or capital.
    • Cost-Effective Setup: One of the most affordable free zones in the UAE, ideal for startups, SMEs, and small businesses looking for cost-effective setup options.
    • Flexible Business Setup Options: HFZ offers flexible business licenses and customized solutions, including single-owner companies and multi-shareholder partnerships.
    • No Currency Restrictions: Businesses can operate in any currency without restrictions, allowing for smoother international transactions.
    • Variety of Business Activities: HFZ caters to a broad spectrum of industries, including trading, manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, and services.
    • Easy Setup Process: Streamlined procedures with a simple and quick application process for business setup and licensing.
    • Warehousing and Industrial Facilities: HFZ is known for its robust infrastructure for warehousing and manufacturing, providing businesses with customized facilities for production, storage, and distribution.
    • Visa and Residency Benefits: HFZ offers visa options for investors, employees, and dependents, with flexibility in the number of visas based on office space and business activity.
    • Access to a Global Network: Businesses in HFZ benefit from access to a large network of international clients, suppliers, and service providers, boosting business growth opportunities.
    • Full Repatriation of Profits and Capital: There are no restrictions on the repatriation of profits, capital, or dividends, offering financial flexibility for investors.
    • No Annual Auditing Requirement for SMEs: Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) are generally not required to submit annual audits, reducing administrative burden.
    • Proximity to UAE Business Hubs: Being close to Dubai and Sharjah offers businesses access to major markets, infrastructure, and clients in the UAE and across the GCC region.
    • International Shipping and Logistics Hub: The proximity to Sharjah Port and other key ports makes HFZ ideal for import/export and logistics companies, with easy access to shipping routes.
