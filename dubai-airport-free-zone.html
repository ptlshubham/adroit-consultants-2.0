Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZA)

  • Home
  • Free Zone Company
  • Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZA)

Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZA) is one of the most prestigious and advanced free zones in the UAE, offering exceptional business services, strategic location near Dubai International Airport, and a world-class infrastructure. It is ideal for industries such as aviation, logistics, technology, and trading.

Steps to Form a Company in Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZA)

1. Select a Business Activity:

  • DAFZA allows a wide range of business activities, including trading, services, logistics, and manufacturing.
  • Ensure the chosen activity is approved by the DAFZA authority.

2. Choose a Legal Structure:

  • Free Zone Establishment (FZE): Single shareholder.
  • Free Zone Company (FZC): Two to 50 shareholders.
  • Branch Office: A branch of an existing local or foreign company.

3. Register a Trade Name:

  • Select a unique trade name that complies with DAFZA’s naming regulations.

4. Submit an Application for Initial Approval:

Provide business details, including:

  • Type of business activity.
  • Legal structure.
  • Shareholder information.

5. Prepare Required Documentation:

  • Passport copies of shareholders and directors.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
  • Proof of residential address (e.g., utility bill).
  • Business plan (for certain activities).
  • No Objection Certificate (NOC) if applicable.

6. Lease Office Space:

DAFZA provides flexible office space solutions, including:

  • Flexi desks.
  • Fully serviced offices.
  • Warehousing facilities for trading and logistics companies.

7. Sign Legal Agreements:

  • Sign the lease agreement and company incorporation documents.

8. Pay the Fees:

  • Pay the license and office setup fees as per DAFZA’s fee structure.

9. Obtain the Business License:

  • Once approved, DAFZA issues the business license along with the Certificate of Incorporation.

10. Open a Corporate Bank Account:

  • Use the issued company documents to open a UAE bank account for your business.

11. Apply for Visas:

  • DAFZA allows visa applications for shareholders, employees, and dependents based on the office size and type.

Advantages of Setting Up a Company in Dubai Airport Free Zone (DAFZA)

  • Strategic Location: Proximity to Dubai International Airport ensures seamless connectivity for logistics and international trade.
  • 100% Foreign Ownership: Full business ownership without requiring a local sponsor.
  • Tax Benefits: 0% corporate tax, personal income tax, and import/export duties.
  • World-Class Infrastructure: State-of-the-art office facilities, warehouses, and logistics support.
  • Customs and Trade Facilitation: Companies benefit from simplified customs procedures and duty-free trade.
  • Ease of Doing Business: Streamlined company setup process with minimal paperwork.
  • Wide Range of Business Activities: From trading and logistics to IT services and consulting.
  • Repatriation of Profits: 100% repatriation of profits and capital.
  • Visa and Residency Solutions: Easy visa issuance for investors, employees, and dependents.
  • No Currency Restrictions: Freedom to conduct business in multiple currencies without restrictions.
  • Global Business Community: Access to an international network of businesses across various sectors.
  • Dedicated Support Services: DAFZA provides support for licensing, visa processing, and operational needs.
  • Flexible Office Options: From virtual desks to large custom-built facilities, tailored to your business size and needs.
  • Access to Key Markets: Ideal location for businesses targeting the Middle East, Africa, and South Asia markets.
  • Recognition and Credibility: A DAFZA-registered company enjoys high credibility in the UAE and globally.
  • No Annual Audit Requirement: Small and medium-sized enterprises are not required to submit annual financial audits.
